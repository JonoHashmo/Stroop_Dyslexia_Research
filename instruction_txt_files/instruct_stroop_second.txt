
Great work! You have completed the first part of the experiment.

During the second part, your job is to indicate which color the word is printed in, regardless of the word itself.

For example, if you see the word RED printed in BLUE ink, the correct answer would be BLUE, because that is the color the word is printed in.

You will have a practice round before the experiment continues.

As a reminder, the keys are:
D = Red
F = Blue
J = Green
K = Yellow

These will also be shown at the bottom of the screen throughout the task.

Press the spacebar when you are ready to begin.
