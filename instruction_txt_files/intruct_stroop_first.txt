
This experiment has two parts. In both parts, you will see words printed on the screen one at a time.

During the first part, your job is to indicate which color the word is printed in, regardless of the word itself.

For example, if you see the word RED printed in BLUE ink, the correct answer would be BLUE, because that is the color the word is printed in.

Use the following keys to respond:
D = Red
F = Blue
J = Green
K = Yellow

A reminder of these keys will be shown at the bottom of the screen during the task.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

Before we begin, you will complete a short practice round so you can get comfortable with the keys.

(Press the spacebar when you are ready to start the practice)
