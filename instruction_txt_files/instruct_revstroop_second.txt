
Great work! You have completed the first part of the experiment.

During the second part, your job is to indicate what word is printed on the screen, regardless of the color it is printed in.

For example, if you see the word RED printed in BLUE ink, the correct answer would be RED, because that is the word that is printed.

You will have a practice round before the experiment continues.

As a reminder, the keys are:
D = Red
F = Blue
J = Green
K = Yellow

These will also be shown at the bottom of the screen throughout the task.

Press the spacebar when you are ready to begin.
